Cauliflower Soup

Description

The cauliflower soup is a comforting blend of tender cauliflower florets, sautéed onions, and garlic, simmered in vegetable broth until soft and velvety. Pureed until smooth, the soup has a rich, silky texture enhanced by the addition of cream or coconut milk for a touch of sweetness. Hints of nutmeg and black pepper add warmth and depth, while a sprinkle of fresh chives or parsley on top adds a pop of color and freshness. Perfect for a cozy meal, this soup is both nourishing and satisfying, making it an ideal choice for any season.

Ingredients

  • cauliflower
  • garlic
  • olive oil
  • heavy cream
  • vegetable broth
  • salt

Recipe

  1. Prepare the Cauliflower:Wash the cauliflower thoroughly and cut it into bite-sized florets.
  2. Sauté the Aromatics:In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Cook the Cauliflower: Add the cauliflower florets to the pot, stirring to coat them with the oil, onions, and garlic. Pour in the vegetable broth, bringing the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat to a simmer and cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the cauliflower is tender.
  4. Blend the Soup: Use an immersion blender to puree the soup directly in the pot until smooth. Alternatively, transfer the soup in batches to a blender, blend until smooth, and then return it to the pot.
  5. Add Cream and Seasoning: Stir in the heavy cream or coconut milk, and season with nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. serving.
